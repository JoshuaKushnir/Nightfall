**Description**
Refactor grass rendering/grid logic out of `HeavenEnvironmentController` into a reusable, config-driven `GrassGrid` module. Keep wind/clouds/post-processing/lens flare in the controller. This enables future environment controllers (Tide forest, Ash wastes, Void rift) to plug in unique foliage configs without duplicating grid/pool/update internals.
